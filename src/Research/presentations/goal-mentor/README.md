# Goal Mentor

我按**公开可检索到、作者列表明确出现“Xing Wang/王幸”**来查了近五年（按 **2021–2026，截至 2026-04-08**）的论文。华西医院官方页面确认，王幸医生是**华西医院胰腺外科/胰腺中心**医生，官方简介写到他已发表**第一作者论文20余篇，其中 SCI 18 篇**。下面这份是我目前能公开核实到的近五年英文论文清单。([wchscu.cn][1])

**2021**

* *Diabetes mellitus induced by immune checkpoint inhibitors* — **Diabetes/Metabolism Research and Reviews**。偏综述/机制与临床总结，刊物层级较高。([PubMed][2])
* *Optimal extent of lymphadenectomy for radical surgery of pancreatic head adenocarcinoma: 2-year survival rate results of single-center, prospective, randomized controlled study* — **Medicine (Baltimore)**。研究设计不错，但期刊本身含金量一般。([PubMed][3])

**2022**

* *Management and outcomes of patients for non-functioning pancreatic neuroendocrine tumours: a multi-institutional analysis* — **ANZ Journal of Surgery**。多中心临床研究，中上。([PubMed][4])
* *Clinicopathological characteristics and survival outcomes of patients with gallbladder neuroendocrine carcinoma: A population-level study* — **Asian Journal of Surgery**。数据库人群研究，中上偏好。([PubMed][5])
* *Prognostic Factors of Small Non-Functional Pancreatic Neuroendocrine Tumors and the Risk of Lymph Node Metastasis: A Population-Level Study* — **Frontiers in Endocrinology**。这篇在小 NF-pNET 风险分层里比较有分量。([Frontiers][6])
* *Prognostic value of preoperative diabetes mellitus in patients with non-functional pancreatic neuroendocrine neoplasms* — **American Journal of Surgery**。临床预后研究，含金量较高。([科学直通车][7])
* *Development and Validation of a Novel Radiomics-Based Nomogram With Machine Learning to Preoperatively Predict Histologic Grade in Pancreatic Neuroendocrine Tumors* — **Frontiers in Oncology**。放射组学/机器学习建模，中等到中上。([PubMed][8])
* *Which Surgeries Are the Best Choice for Chronic Pancreatitis: A Network Meta-Analysis of Randomized Controlled Trials* — **Frontiers in Surgery**。证据综合，期刊层级中等。([Frontiers][9])

**2023**

* *Associations between Systemic Immune-Inflammation Index and Diabetes Mellitus Secondary to Pancreatic Ductal Adenocarcinoma* — **Journal of Clinical Medicine**。临床指标研究，中上。([PubMed][10])
* *An Orthotopic Resection Surgical Technique Using Biologic Meshes for Repair Following Pancreaticoduodenectomy in a Patient With a Large Duodenal Gastrointestinal Stromal Tumor: A Case Report* — **Journal of Clinical Medicine**。这是**病例报告**，不宜和原创临床研究按同一档看。([PubMed][11])
* *Clinicopathological features and long-term prognosis of purely cystic pancreatic neuroendocrine tumors: A single-center experience* — **Asian Journal of Surgery**。围绕 pNET 的连续产出之一，中上。([科学直通车][12])
* *Prognostic role and predictors of lymph node involvement in pancreatic neuroendocrine tumors* — **European Journal of Radiology**。这篇质量不错，影像与淋巴结转移预测方向较强。([PubMed][13])
* *The differential effects of sarcopenia and cachexia on overall survival in pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma patients undergoing radical excision: A multicenter retrospective cohort study* — **Cancer Medicine**。多中心队列，临床价值较好。([PubMed][14])

**2024**

* *Clusterin is upregulated by erastin, a ferroptosis inducer and exerts cytoprotective effects in pancreatic adenocarcinoma cells* — **Anti-Cancer Drugs**。偏基础/转化，期刊层级中等。([PubMed][15])
* *Prognostic significance of systemic immune-inflammation index in patients with nonfunction pancreatic neuroendocrine tumor undergoing surgical resection* — **Cancer Medicine**。pNET 预后方向，含金量较好。([PubMed][16])
* *A nomogram to preoperatively predict the aggressiveness of non-functional pancreatic neuroendocrine tumors based on CT features* — **European Journal of Radiology**。这篇很值得重视，和你现在做的 NF-pNET 影像预测非常接近。([PubMed][17])

**2025**

* *Prognosis of small pancreatic neuroendocrine neoplasms: Functionality matters* — **American Journal of Surgery**。小 pNEN 预后主题，期刊和题目都不错。([PubMed][18])
* *Predictors Based on the Radiologic Characteristics for Aggressiveness of Small (< 20 mm) Nonfunctioning Pancreatic Neuroendocrine Tumors* — **Journal of Surgical Oncology**。也是你这个方向的核心相关文献之一。([PubMed][19])
* *A nomogram to preoperatively predict the aggressiveness of pancreatic neuroendocrine tumors based on CT features and 3D CT radiomic features* — **Abdominal Radiology**。影像+3D radiomics，方法学上有看点。([Springer Link][20])
* *Non-hypervascular pancreatic neuroendocrine neoplasms differentiation from CA19-9 negative pancreatic ductal adenocarcinomas based on contrast CT: A large sample series* — **European Journal of Radiology**。鉴别诊断方向，样本量和临床实用性都比较好。([科学直通车][21])

**2026**

* *Qualitative and quantitative analysis of enhanced computed tomography in differentiating and grading pancreatic neuroendocrine neoplasms: a multicenter study* — **Abdominal Radiology**。最新一篇，继续沿着 pNEN 影像定量分析这条线走。([Springer Link][22])

我对这些论文的**含金量**判断，大概是这样：

**第一梯队，比较有分量**

* **American Journal of Surgery**
* **European Journal of Radiology**
* **Cancer Medicine**
* **Diabetes/Metabolism Research and Reviews**
* **Frontiers in Endocrinology**

这些刊物当前公开指标里整体都不低：比如 **Diabetes/Metabolism Research and Reviews** 当前 JIF 约 **6.0**，**European Journal of Radiology** JIF 约 **3.3**，**American Journal of Surgery** JIF 约 **2.7**，**Frontiers in Endocrinology** 近年 JCR IF 约 **3.9**，**Cancer Medicine** 近年影响力也在肿瘤综合刊里处于不错的位置。([期刊搜索][23])

**第二梯队，中上**

* **Abdominal Radiology**
* **Journal of Surgical Oncology**
* **Asian Journal of Surgery**
* **Journal of Clinical Medicine**
* **ANZ Journal of Surgery**

这类期刊在各自专科圈里有认可度，尤其适合影像、外科肿瘤、亚专科问题，但离顶刊还有距离。当前公开信息里，**Abdominal Radiology** JIF 约 **2.2**，**Asian Journal of Surgery** JIF 约 **3.8**，表现都不差。([Web of Science Journal][24])

**第三梯队，中等**

* **Medicine (Baltimore)**
* **Frontiers in Surgery**
* **Frontiers in Oncology**
* **Anti-Cancer Drugs**

这些刊能发出来当然也说明有产出，但单篇“期刊含金量”一般弱于上面两档。**Medicine** 的 SJR 指标相对一般，**Anti-Cancer Drugs** 当前公开指标也偏中下。([Resurchify][25])

我给你的**总体判断**是：

王幸医生近五年的论文不是零散发，而是有一条非常清楚的主线：**胰腺神经内分泌肿瘤，尤其是 NF-pNET / small pNET 的术前影像评估、侵袭性预测、淋巴结转移与预后分层**；其次才是 PDAC、围手术期问题、炎症/代谢指标等。这个特点说明他在**胰腺外科 + pNET 影像风险分层**这个小方向上是持续深耕的。([Frontiers][6])

所以，若你问“**在华西胰腺外科年轻医生/骨干里，这个科研履历怎么样**”，我的评价是：**不错，方向聚焦，连续性强，尤其在 pNET 这一小方向有辨识度**。
但若你问“**是不是已经到 Annals of Surgery、JAMA Surgery、Gut、Lancet Oncology 那种顶刊级履历**”，那我这次公开检索到的近五年条目里，**暂时没有看到这一层级**。因此更准确的说法是：**中上偏强、亚专科特色鲜明，但还不是顶刊横扫型**。([wchscu.cn][1])

对你最有参考价值的几篇，我会优先看这 5 篇：

1. **2024, European Journal of Radiology**：NF-pNET aggressiveness CT nomogram。([PubMed][17])
2. **2025, Journal of Surgical Oncology**：small NF-pNET radiologic predictors。([PubMed][19])
3. **2025, Abdominal Radiology**：CT + 3D radiomics nomogram for pNET aggressiveness。([Springer Link][20])
4. **2023, European Journal of Radiology**：lymph node involvement predictors in pNET。([PubMed][13])
5. **2022, Frontiers in Endocrinology**：small NF-pNET 与淋巴结转移风险。([Frontiers][6])

这份清单已经够你做初步判断了。接下来我可以直接继续给你做一版**“按作者位次 + 文章类型 + 期刊档次 + 对你课题的参考价值”**的更实用排序版。
